Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our CCTV, security and solar installation services

What types of CCTV cameras do you install?

We install Analog, IP (Network), and WiFi CCTV cameras. Analog cameras are budget-friendly for basic surveillance. IP cameras offer high-resolution video quality and network-based monitoring. WiFi cameras provide wireless installation with easy mobile app access. We'll recommend the best option based on your property and requirements.

Do you install attendance machines for offices?

Yes! We install biometric attendance machines with fingerprint, face recognition, and RFID card options. Our service includes complete setup, software configuration, user enrollment, timing rules setup, and staff training. We provide attendance systems for offices, schools, factories, and businesses in Rawalpindi & Islamabad.

Can you install smart door locks with access control?

Absolutely! We install smart door locks with biometric, card, and PIN access options. Our door lock installation includes magnetic and electronic locks, user access management, integration with CCTV systems, and mobile app control. Perfect for securing rooms, shops, offices, and main entrances.

Do you provide fire alarm system installation?

Yes! We install complete fire alarm systems with smoke detectors, heat detectors, sirens, and control panels. Our fire alarm installation ensures early fire warnings to protect lives and property. We provide professional installation for shops, offices, warehouses, and buildings with proper testing and certification.

Can solar panels power security systems during load-shedding?

Absolutely! We install on-grid and hybrid solar systems specifically designed to keep CCTV cameras, WiFi routers, attendance machines, door locks, and all security systems running during power outages. Solar backup ensures continuous operation even during load-shedding, protecting your property 24/7.
